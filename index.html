Hello! I'm Vera, and I like data.

I started getting interested in statistics and data while I was studying for my Master's degree in Special Education. I stayed interested when I became a special education teacher, and added a little coding into the mix when I shifted into psychometric and educational testing. In 2018, I decided to make a career switch to data analysis and worked hard to win a scholarship to Udacity's Data Analyst Nanodegree program.

Now that I've graduated from the program, I'm looking for full-time remote data analysis work that still lets me do some good in the world. In the meantime, I'm continuing to study and research, both to develop my own skills and to learn more about the education, disability, and health fields. Check out my portfolio below!
